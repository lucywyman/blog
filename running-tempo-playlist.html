Running Tempo Playlist

03.10.2018

I've always loved to run. It helps me clear my mind, makes my body feel good, and that endorphin rush just can't be beat. However, since I typically run early in the morning and listen to murder podcasts I opt to use the elliptical at the gym in the mornings in place of running. That said, this playlist is definitely applicable to whatever cardio you're doing: running, elliptical, even biking! I have these in a spotify playlist (which gets updated frequently as I get bored of songs), and generally just hit 'shuffle', but you could definitely construct a more structured workout if you'd like. Anywho, finding tempo songs that I like to workout to is a hard task, and I wanted to provide my own list of songs that work for me and my pace (~9 minute miles). Enjoy!

Most of these songs also make great sprints! I sprint during the chorus, when the songs tend to get faster.
