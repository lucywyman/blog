Why I'm Not Afraid of Self-Driving Cars

02.26.2016

Among the ranks of podcasts NPR creates, one is an aggregated feed of technical bits from a variety of news podcasts -- basically a curated, audio Hacker News. This past week, everyone has been FREAKING OUT about the WFC fight of the century, Apple vs. FBI, in a non-stop stream of uninformed (or worse, half-informed) newscasters questioning whether Apple is doing the right thing by protecting their users privacy*. However, in the breaths between FREAKING OUT, there were a few bits on self-driving cars, and whether America is ready for them. The bit focused on folks saying they didn't trust self-driving cars

  • I'll spare you the drama and to say I side very strongly with Apple, and if you don't care to read more about the issue you should as well. Take it from an engineer.
